TRAVELS GOALS ABOUT ME

I have always been the kind of person to live fully in the moment. I was never too caught up in what was going to happen or what I was going to end up doing. Which as a young adult, may not be the best quality. I never knew what I really wanted to do with my life, just that I simply wanted to live it. Once I got to college I struggled with this. I still did not know what I wanted to do, but everyone was forcing me to decide. I had a couple ideas that I might like, but none that I could dedicate my entire existence too.

My sophomore year in college, I found this volunteer abroad program. GIVE, was a program that went to third world countries and aided them in any way that the country needed. I have always been interested in traveling the world, and something like this seemed like a fun idea. I went to Thailand and mostly stayed in the Northern jungles with the Hill Tribe people. These were indigenous people, technically not Thai citizens. For the next three weeks I built fresh water tanks, new roads, taught english, learned about their culture, and made life long friends.
